THE ART OF LANDSCAPE DESIGN

Originally trained in decorative and performing arts, Franchesca retrained as a landscape designer in her early twenties. Based in Cape Town, she has undertaken private and commercial gardens all over South Africa, and also in Europe, UK, Australia, and Seychelles.
